HOW TO PLAY


In Eggforce, you control a nest of Eggoas who produce resources (RADs) and fight for land.

Your goal is to harvest as many RADs as possible. At the end of the round, you win a POA reward. This reward is proportional to your harvested RADs, compared to the harvested RADs of every player.

JOINING THE GAME


Select one of the four Tribes first, then join the game.
Members of each Tribe cannot attack each other. Every time a member of your Tribe spends POA on the game, you receive some extra RADs through the Tribe Chest.

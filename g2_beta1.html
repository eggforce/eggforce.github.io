HOW TO PLAY


G2 is a King of the Hill game. Sit on the Throne for as long as possible.

The longer you sit, the more Glory you get. Glory can be traded for POA at the end of the game.

PUSH THE KING


To join the game, you must first Push the King.

A Push costs POA. The previous King gains Glory for the amount of time he sat on the Throne, and loses his Throne. You become the new King.

Because everyone likes a good Push, 200 Eggoas immediately join your nest.

You can Push again even after you've joined the game.

HATCH SHROOMS


While you aren't King, your Eggoas produce Shrooms passively.

Hatch your Shrooms at any point into more Eggoas. The more Eggoas, the more Shroom production.

Eggoas don't produce Shrooms while you sit on the Throne. They're too busy guarding your Kingdom.

PULL THE KING


Instead of Pushing, you can sacrifice Eggoas to Pull the King off the throne.

Just like Push, Pull gives the previous King his Glory, then you become the King.

To Pull, you need to sacrifice more Eggoas than the current Throne Army. The Throne Army is either the amount of Eggoas the previous King used to Pull, or 200 if he used Push.

MISCELLANEOUS


- Glory accumulates faster at the beginning of the game, then follows a decreasing curve.

- Push cost starts high, then decreases proportionally to the number of previous Pushes.

- Shroom production receives a slight multiplier the longer you don't hatch.
